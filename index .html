About Us

Vardhman Mahaveer Health Care is a premier multi-specialty hospital located in the heart of Patiala. With a patient-centric approach and a team of highly skilled medical professionals, we are committed to providing comprehensive, personalized, and compassionate care to our community. Our state-of-the-art facilities and a wide range of specialized services ensure that we meet the diverse healthcare needs of our patients with the utmost dedication and excellence.

Our Services

Cardiology

Comprehensive care for heart and vascular health, including diagnostic and interventional procedures.

Orthopedics

Expert treatment for bone, joint, and muscle conditions, from sports injuries to joint replacement surgery.

Obstetrics & Gynaecology

Providing comprehensive care for women's health, including maternity and reproductive services.

Dentistry

Advanced dental care, from general dentistry to oral and maxillofacial surgery.

General Surgery

Skilled surgical team for a wide range of procedures, ensuring patient safety and effective outcomes.

Neurology

Diagnosis and treatment of disorders affecting the nervous system, including the brain and spine.
